Chocolate chip cookies

Mix 1 cup of butter with 150 grams of brown sugar and 50 grams of white sugar until it looks yummy
Add 1 egg and 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla and mix until it looks even more yummy
Add 312 grams of flour and 1/2 teaspoon of baking soda. Mix with a spoon until hegemony
Add a lot of chocolate chips and fold in with the spoon
